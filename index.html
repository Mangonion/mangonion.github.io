A Brief History

Smosh's Youtube Channel

Every story has a beginning.And for these two friends, they had a pretty good one. Ian Hecox and Anthony Padilla are the leaders od smosh, having met in college. Anthony was into coding and after taking a classs designed a website called smosh.com. It was mainly a social space like twitter, facebook and other things like that.
When ian and anthony made a video of them lip syncing to the power rangers theme, it went very popular. They had it on their website, and only on their website, they would have to pay for every view. After a while, it started getting very expensive for a college student, so they had to stop posting their lip sync videos.
Eventually, they found out about YouTube. noy only was it a platform that they could continue posting their videos, it was free!

After their phase of posting lip sync videos, they started getting into sketch comedy. They would post daily short sketches and other types of sketches. They started gaining a following on their channel, and very quickly. They ventured into gaming channel, and a second channel for games and behind the scenes. They got a host company called defy media to make their production value much higher, whichc introduced a ton of new cast members including Shayne Topp, Olivia Sui, Courtney Miller, Keith Leak Jr.,And Noah Grossman. On the smosh games side of things, Damien Haas, Joshua Ovenshire, Mari Takahashi, David Moss, Ericka Bozeman, Amra Ricketts, Wesley Johnson, and Matthew Sohinki.

Missing: Matthew Sohinki, David Moss, Damien Haas.
